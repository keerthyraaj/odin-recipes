Lasagna

Lasagna is a type of pasta dish that is popular in Italy and other parts of the world.

It is made by layering sheets of pasta with various fillings, such as meat, cheese, and vegetables, and then baking it in the oven.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Heat the tomato sauce in a pan.
  4. Layer the pasta, meat, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 30-40 minutes until golden brown.
